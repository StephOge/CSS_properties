How CSS is important:
What is the importance of CSS in today's society? CSS is important because it controls all design-related aspects of your website. Typography, colours, page layouts and any other visual aspects of your website are all controlled by CSS.

The difference between paddings, margins and borders:
Padding - The inner space between the content and the border of your box.
Border - The perimeter of the box. Borders can be invisible or they could be a thick colored line
Margin - The outer space (or lack of space) surrounding the box.
